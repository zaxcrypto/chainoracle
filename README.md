## Onchain ChainOracle 🔮

ChainOracle is a simple on-chain oracle experience that reveals a projected 2026 wallet outcome based on your current address

## Features:
* Enter any EVM Or Sol wallet address
* Reads on-chain signals
* Generates a 2026 wealth projection
* Clean and minimal UI

Live Link:
https://chainoracle-beta.vercel.app

## Usage:
1. Enter your wallet address
2. Reveal your oracle
3. View your 2026 projection

## 📌 Note

This is a conceptual oracle experience and not financial advice.
